Platform purpose

Career guidance that moves from confusion to action.

This platform is designed for students, graduates, early-career professionals and career changers who want to understand commerce career options and build a realistic next step. It is structured around interests, strengths, work preferences and direction, not only qualifications.

South African context

Explore careers connected to JSE-listed companies, banks, retailers, mining houses, telecoms, FMCG, logistics, manufacturing, public sector entities, DFIs, audit firms, SMEs, startups and NGOs.

Global relevance

Many of these skills transfer into Africa, Europe, the United States, the Middle East, multinational companies, global banks, consulting firms, international NGOs and development institutions.

Future readiness

AI, automation, ESG, analytics and regulation are changing commerce careers. The platform highlights what may be automated and what human skills will become more valuable.

Future of commerce careers

What may change over the next 10 years.

Routine production will become faster. Human value will move toward judgement, ethics, communication, problem framing, stakeholder trust and strategic decision-making.

Automated

Basic reconciliations, repetitive reports, first-draft commentary, standard checks and dashboard refreshes.

Augmented

Forecasting, scenario planning, risk analysis, valuation support, policy research and performance diagnosis.

More human

Ethical judgement, leadership, negotiation, influence, ambiguity tolerance and strategic communication.

Learn now

Excel, Power BI, SQL basics, financial modelling, business writing, AI prompting, ESG literacy and portfolio projects.
