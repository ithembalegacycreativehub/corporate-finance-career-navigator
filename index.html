Refined build brief

From static guide to career discovery platform.

The upgraded site is designed as an offline learning product for students, graduates and early-career professionals. It combines role education, case-based learning, future-of-finance thinking, dynamic assessments and practical preparation plans.

Instructional direction

Each role page teaches what the role does, why it matters, how work is organized, what skills are needed, who thrives, who may struggle, and how to prepare with portfolio projects.

Career strategy direction

The assessments test motivation, work style, quantitative appetite, ambiguity tolerance, detail orientation, stakeholder preference and future-readiness rather than simple interest.

Technical direction

The platform uses only HTML, CSS and JavaScript. It runs by opening index.html in a browser and does not need internet access, accounts, APIs or a server.

Future of finance

What corporate finance could look like in 10 years.

Automation will absorb repetitive reporting and reconciliation. The human premium will move toward judgment, narrative, business partnering, governance, ethical use of AI, scenario design and strategic capital allocation.

Automated

Routine production

Standard variance packs, reconciliations, data cleansing, first-draft commentary, transaction screening and basic dashboarding will increasingly be AI-assisted.

Augmented

Analysis and decision support

Finance professionals will use AI copilots to test scenarios, identify anomalies, interrogate large datasets and prepare stronger narratives faster.

More valuable

Human judgment

Stakeholder trust, commercial courage, ethics, negotiation, risk judgment, strategic framing and clarity under uncertainty will become more important.

Assessment memo

How the scoring works.

Role assessments use weighted answers from 0 to 3. Higher-scoring answers indicate stronger alignment with the role's motivation, work style, technical appetite, pressure pattern and future skills. The consolidated assessment maps each answer to one or more finance roles, then ranks the strongest matches.

Role-specific fit

Questions test whether the user is energized by the actual work of the role. Results include suitability, strengths, development areas, projects, related roles and a 6-month plan.

Dynamic question banks

Every role has a larger pool than the 10 questions displayed. JavaScript shuffles the bank and selects a fresh set, creating many possible assessment versions offline.

Career matching

The final assessment compares roles against each other. It identifies top matches, weaker matches and practical next steps for internships, graduate roles or entry-level applications.
